What is the eligibility criteria to open an account?
To open an account, you must be at least 18 years old and have a valid government-issued ID.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Change Personal Information' button on the settings page and follow the instructions.
Can I have more than 1 accounts for investing?
No, every individual is entitled to only 1 account under their name.
